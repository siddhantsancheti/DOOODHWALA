DOOODHWALA — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 1 June 2026

This page explains how to delete your DOOODHWALA account and the data associated with it. DOOODHWALA is operated for the milk-delivery app published under the package name com.dooodhwala.app.

Option 1 — Delete in the app

  1. Open DOOODHWALA and sign in.
  2. Go to your Profile / Settings.
  3. Choose Delete Account and confirm.

Your account and personal data are removed as described below.

Option 2 — Request by email

Send an email to support@dooodhwala.com from your registered email, or include your registered phone number, with the subject "Delete my account". We will verify and process your request within 30 days.

What gets deleted

DeletedRetained (and for how long)
Your profile (name, phone, address, photo), chat messages and shared media, orders, subscriptions, push tokens, and assignment to a dairyman. Financial/transaction records may be retained where required by law or tax regulation, in anonymised or minimal form, typically up to the statutory retention period. Backups are purged on a rolling cycle.
