DOOODHWALA Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

DOOODHWALA ("we", "us", "our") operates the DOOODHWALA mobile application and website (the "Service"), which connects customers with local milk-delivery vendors ("dairymen" / "milkmen") for daily dairy ordering, delivery tracking, and payments. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. By using the Service you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide

DataWhy we collect it
Mobile phone numberAccount creation and sign-in via one-time password (OTP). This is your primary identifier.
NameTo display you to your assigned dairyman and on bills/receipts.
Delivery addressTo match you with nearby dairymen and enable delivery.
Profile photo (optional)Shown in your profile and chat.
Orders, subscriptions & preferencesTo process daily/recurring milk orders and generate monthly bills.
Chat messages, photos, documents & shared locationsTo enable communication between you and your dairyman.

b) Information collected automatically

DataWhy
Location (precise, foreground and—when you grant it—background)To show nearby dairymen, enable live delivery tracking, and let dairymen navigate to you. Background location is used only to update delivery progress on an active order.
Device push token (Firebase Cloud Messaging)To send order, delivery and payment notifications.
App diagnostics / crash dataTo keep the app stable and secure.

c) Payment information

Online payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not collect or store your full card numbers, UPI PIN, or banking credentials — these are handled directly by Razorpay under their own security and PCI-DSS compliance. We retain only a payment reference, amount, status and the related order/bill identifiers to confirm your payment. For Cash on Delivery, we generate a one-time confirmation OTP.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and secure your account (phone OTP authentication).
  • Match you with dairymen, place and fulfil orders, and track deliveries.
  • Generate monthly bills and process payments.
  • Send transactional notifications (orders, delivery status, bills).
  • Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
  • Detect, prevent and address fraud, abuse and technical issues.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for third-party advertising, and we do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal/tax obligations, resolve disputes and enforce agreements. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymise your personal data, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial records).

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access / correction: update your name, address and profile in the app.
  • Account deletion: you may delete your account from within the app or by contacting us; this removes your personal data as described above.
  • Permissions: you can revoke location, camera, photo and notification permissions in your device settings at any time (some features may stop working).
  • Notifications: opt out of push notifications via device settings.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), OTP-based authentication, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and notify you of material breaches as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

DOOODHWALA is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised version here with a new "Last updated" date, and where appropriate notify you in the app.
